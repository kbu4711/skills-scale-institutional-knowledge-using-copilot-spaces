# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

This directory is the entry point for OctoAcme project management documentation. Use it to onboard quickly, understand who owns what, and follow the delivery lifecycle from idea to continuous improvement.

## Process Overview

OctoAcme follows a lifecycle that starts with **initiation** (business need, goals, and stakeholders), moves into **planning** (backlog, estimates, milestones, and dependencies), then into **execution and tracking** (delivery rhythm, quality checks, and progress reporting). Work then proceeds through **release and deployment** (pre-release readiness, deployment, and rollback planning), and closes with **retrospectives and continuous improvement** to turn lessons learned into concrete actions for future iterations. Across all stages, risk management, communication, and clearly defined roles support consistent delivery.
